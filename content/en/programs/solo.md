## Tres territorios
**Baroque Guitar and Theorbo**

An exploration of three distinct musical territories connected by the baroque guitar and theorbo. This program traces the development and spread of these instruments across European courts and cultural centers, highlighting regional styles and compositional approaches.

### Program Highlights
- Italian baroque guitar repertoire
- Spanish influences
- Cross-cultural musical exchanges

## Dos peninsulas
**Renaissance Lute and Baroque Guitar**

A musical dialogue between the Iberian and Italian peninsulas, showcasing how these two great cultural centers influenced each other through their lute and guitar traditions. The program presents contrasting styles, rhythms, and compositional techniques from both regions.

### Musical Bridges
- Spanish vihuela and guitar works
- Italian lute masterpieces
- Cross-Peninsula influences

## Una storia fra le corde...
**Vihuela and Theorbo**

"A story among the strings" - this program weaves together narratives through the intimate sound of the vihuela and the resonant depths of the theorbo. Spanning from the Spanish Golden Age to the Italian Baroque, the program tells musical stories of love, devotion, and human experience.

### Repertoire Themes
- Spanish Golden Age vihuela music
- Italian theorbo works
- Narrative and descriptive pieces

---

## Tanyer de gala
**Vihuela and Baroque Guitar**

A festive program celebrating the courtly and popular traditions of Spain through the vihuela and baroque guitar. "Tanyer" refers to the act of playing stringed instruments, and "gala" evokes celebration and refinement - together presenting the full spectrum of Spanish plucked string music.

### Program Character
- Courtly dances and variations
- Popular melodies and arrangements
- Virtuosic displays
