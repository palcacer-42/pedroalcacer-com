## Mecatl — Harmonium

**Historical Lute Instruments**

<div class="program-description-block">

**Mecatl** — in Nahuatl, the ancient language of the Aztecs, means *"string"*. **Harmonium** — from the Latin *harmonia*: harmony, accord, consonance.

Pedro and Hugo are two friends and colleagues who happened to grow up and study in the same neighbourhood and at the same university in one of the largest cities in the world: Mexico City. They share not only their birthplace and alma mater, but also a lifelong passion for Renaissance and Baroque music, and the cultural heritage shaped by a country with an enormous musical and artistic richness.

> *"Plucked instruments are neither perfect nor imperfect, but rather as one plays them…"*
> — Gaspar Sanz, 1692
