## About Pedro Alcàcer

Pedro Alcàcer is a professional lutenist and early music specialist with extensive experience performing Renaissance and Baroque music on period instruments.

### Professional Background

With decades of experience in historical performance, Pedro specializes in:

- **Lute** - Renaissance and Baroque
- **Theorbo** - Baroque accompaniment
- **Vihuela** - Spanish Renaissance
- **Arciliuto** - Baroque lute variant

### Performance Experience

Pedro has performed at major international festivals and concert halls, bringing authentic historical performance to contemporary audiences.

### Teaching

In addition to his performance career, Pedro offers masterclasses and workshops on early music instruments and historical performance practice.

### Recognition

His interpretation of Luis Milán's El Maestro has been recognized internationally, and he continues to explore and document early music traditions.
