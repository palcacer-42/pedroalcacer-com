Concert Programs

Pedro Alcàcer performs a diverse repertoire spanning from the Renaissance to the Baroque period on historical plucked string instruments. His programs explore the rich musical traditions of Europe through carefully curated selections that showcase the unique character of each instrument.

Solo Recitals

Intimate programs featuring lute, theorbo, vihuela, and baroque guitar, presenting the masterworks of the Renaissance and Baroque periods. Each program is designed to take the audience on a journey through different musical landscapes, regional styles, and cultural exchanges.

Chamber Music

Collaborative performances with distinguished early music ensembles, bringing to life the chamber music repertoire of the 16th and 17th centuries. These programs feature a variety of instrumental and vocal combinations, from intimate duos to larger chamber groups.
